    • The Weight of Me I can see that no matter the action, I’m left with dissatisfaction If I do, then I live in fear of me If I don’t, I’ll wonder who I’ll be If I do, my life is this If I don’t, I could live in bliss Bliss but still unsatisfied Only I can be my guide Either pick and crush my lover’s breath Or choose my own death How much must I sacrifice For me to be happy with my life? If I choose it- will it fit? My heart; a gaping pit I’m left with no solution No quick and easy resolution When I build myself will I remain in pain? For the rest of my life just picking up the pieces in vain
