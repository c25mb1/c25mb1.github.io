I see “the steps already marked” (Shelley 49) But when I'm done, what's my mark? What's your mark? The me in the mirror? Are you who I am, or who I want to be? What do I want to become of me? And though I can see you I wonder what you’ve been through? You yell, “I will pioneer a new way” (Shelley 49) But what if after I’ve done the math What if me…you still come to this bloodbath? And what new pain would take over this wrath? That will replace when I find this new path After I'm done will the me in the mirror even be okay? “Explore unknown powers” (Shelley 49) you say By what means are you going to defy Some rules are set in place not to ask why What makes me… you special to not comply “Unfold to the world” (Shelley 49) what I have to give But is it more for the world or for me to forgive? Myself and allow me to grow Myself and really learn to know Myself or you in the mirror
